If you wish to opt-out of personalized advertising or delete your data from these networks, you can easily do so by resetting your advertising ID directly from your device’s settings (e.g., through Google Settings on Android or Apple Settings on iOS).

For more information on how ironSource handles data, please review the ironSource Privacy Policy.
